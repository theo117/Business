How It Works

The monthly plan is tailored around the project, not forced into a generic subscription model.

What the monthly plan is

The monthly website plan is a structured arrangement for businesses that want a more complete website without carrying the full project cost upfront in one payment.

It can combine the website build itself with support, maintenance, technical updates, and selected ongoing improvements depending on the agreed scope, support expectations, and delivery structure.

What it is based on

  • What the business needs the website to achieve
  • Page count and website complexity
  • Platform choice and technical features
  • Content and brand-readiness
  • Level of support required
  • Whether ongoing updates are included
  • The agreed delivery term

Important Notes

The final structure is confirmed before work begins

Every monthly plan is quoted per project

The final amount depends on scope, features, support level, and the agreed project term.

Scope still matters

A monthly plan does not mean unlimited design or development. What is included is clearly outlined before the project starts.

Formal terms are provided separately

If a monthly plan is the right fit, you will receive the detailed terms covering inclusions, responsibilities, and the delivery arrangement.

A qualification form is part of the process

For monthly-plan enquiries, a follow-up questionnaire is used to understand the scope, content readiness, support needs, and delivery expectations before a tailored quote is prepared.
