# Enquiry Response Templates

These templates are designed for the current free Web3Forms workflow so replies can be sent manually with minimal editing.

## 1. Monthly Plan Enquiry

**Subject**

`Monthly Website Plan Follow-Up | Teodor Web Solutions`

**Email**

```text
Hi [Name],

Thank you for your enquiry.

Based on your selection, here is the Monthly Website Plan Overview:
https://www.theodorenelson.co.za/monthly-plan-guide.html

To help us understand the project properly, please complete the qualification form here:
https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeLJJBFGW5cLMWCRofuarrPMCMuj5Tm8bD7e64GitC-KL9NLw/viewform?usp=dialog

Once we have reviewed your enquiry and qualification details, we can recommend the most suitable structure for your project and prepare the right next step.

Kind regards,
Teodor Web Solutions
```

## 4. Short Internal Checklist Before Replying

Before sending the reply, quickly check:

1. Which `Project Model` the client selected
2. Whether the enquiry sounds like a larger or more involved website
3. Whether the monthly-plan guide should be included
4. Whether the Google qualification form should be included
5. Whether the lead needs a quick call before quoting

## 5. Suggested Copy-Paste Shortcuts

You can save these labels in your own notes or email drafts:

- `Monthly plan response`
- `Once-off response`
- `Not sure response`

That way your manual free-plan workflow stays fast and consistent.
