# Google Form Update Guide

This file is an update guide for your existing Google Form, not a rebuild from scratch.

Your form is already a strong `Web Design Client Brief`. The aim here is to improve it for monthly-plan qualification without losing the useful design-brief detail you already have.

## Current Form Title

`Web Design Client Brief`

## Add These Questions For Monthly-Plan Qualification

### 1. Why are you interested in a monthly website plan?

- Type: `Paragraph`
- Required: `Yes`

Why:

- this tells you whether the client is motivated by lower upfront cost, ongoing support, or both

### 2. Do you expect ongoing support after launch?

- Type: `Multiple choice`
- Required: `Yes`

Options:

- Yes
- Maybe
- No

### 3. What kind of ongoing support do you expect?

- Type: `Checkboxes`
- Required: `No`

Options:

- Technical updates
- Content edits
- Design improvements
- Landing page additions
- Advice and guidance
- Not sure yet

## Remove This Question

### Number of revision rounds included

Recommendation:

- remove it from the form

Reason:

- this is better handled in your quote or agreement
- it sounds like an internal pricing/contract decision, not a client brief question

## Suggested Order

Use this general order:

1. business basics
2. current brand and links
3. website goals
4. design direction
5. pages and features
6. platform and hosting
7. monthly-plan qualification
8. budget and timeline

## Recommended Form Settings

- Collect email addresses: `On`
- Show progress bar: `On`
- Limit to 1 response: `Off`
