What We Focus On

Credibility, structure, and presentation that supports the business

What teodor dev tech focuses on

teodor dev tech is built around a simple idea: many businesses do not need a flashy website, they need a credible one. The site should explain what the business does, make the company look dependable, and guide visitors toward an enquiry, quote, or booking.

That means every project is approached as a business asset. The work prioritises clear structure, responsive design, practical calls to action, and a presentation that feels aligned with a serious company.

Core services

  • Business website design
  • WordPress development
  • Website redesigns and upgrades
  • Lead-focused page structure
  • Professional presentation for service businesses
Approach

Every project starts with what the business needs to communicate

How projects are approached

Each website starts with positioning. The goal is to understand what the business does, who it serves, and what the website needs to communicate immediately. From there, the design direction and page structure are shaped around trust, clarity, and conversion.

Whether the build is done in WordPress or with a custom front end, the priority stays the same: give the business a cleaner, more confident presence online.

What clients can expect

  • Clear communication about scope and website goals
  • Responsive layouts that work across devices
  • Business-focused page structure and calls to action
  • Practical support after launch
  • Quotes shaped around real project needs, not guesswork
